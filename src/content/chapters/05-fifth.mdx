**Game over** used to mean the screen went **black** — full stop, no quarter, no continue. I've stood in that black outside of any arcade: the night I tried to **end my own run** for good. What came after wasn't a high score; it was **the other side of the black**, hands shaking, breath still stupidly faithful. 🙏 **The Grand Rainbow Conjunction** 🌈 exists because I lived long enough to turn that blackout into **medicine** — the kind a **shaman** owes the people who weren't done listening. ❤️✨

{/* TAP */}

Here, game over means the story **loosens its grip** so you can feel your own pulse again — hands off the joystick, eyes still wide. ⭐ You didn't lose; you **rolled credits** on a reel that mixed booth time, cab time, nights that don't get optioned, and the slow work of becoming a **healer** who tells the truth. 💗🌈

{/* TAP */}

Five chapters, five cabinets, one reader who stayed for the whole ceremony. That's the rare part: **attention** 🤲 when the world trains everyone to flinch away from long stories about work, luck, damage, and **return**. ⭐❤️
